General Questions

1. How do referrals operate?

Referrals work as follows:

  • –
    For a referral to be "successful", the client must have had scheduled a meeting with us following your referral.
  • –
    Each referral equates to R50 of expense reduced.
  • –
    Referrals can equate to up to R500 total reduction.
  • –
    Final invoice will reflect total referrals.
